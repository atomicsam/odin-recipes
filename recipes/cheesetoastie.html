Cheese Toastie Recipe

Description

This is a recipe that will be used to make your very own cheeae toastie

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

Follow these steps to make your very own cheese toastie

  1. Get two slices of bread and butter either sides of these slices of bread
  2. Get the cheese and grate it using a chees grater
  3. Using the grated cheese sprinkle as much cheese as you desire on to the slice of bread
  4. Cover up the slice of bread with another slice of bread (both slices of bread should be buttered
  5. Place these slices of bread with cheese into the grill
  6. Turn on the grill to medium settings and then wait 15 minutes
  7. Take out the slices of bread and enjoy
