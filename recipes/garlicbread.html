Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

Garlic Bread is a beautiful type of bread that will complement any soup, try the recipe below to make your own.

Ingredients

  • Part-baked French Baguette
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Garlic
  • Pinch of salt
  • Parsley
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

Follow these steps to make the perfect garlic bread.

  1. Make sure the garlic you are using is smooth by spreading it into a pinch of salt
  2. Mix the garlic, salt, parsley, black pepper, olive oil and unsalted butter together
  3. Mix until smooth
  4. Slice the baguette into 12 pieces
  5. With a knife spread the garlic butter down the slits, wrap in foil and bake for 10 minutes
  6. Unwrap and bake again until you get your desired texture
